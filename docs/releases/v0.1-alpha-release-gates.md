# StateMotion v0.1 Alpha — Release Gates

Three severity levels. **Private Alpha cannot launch with open BLOCKER issues.**
MAJOR issues require explicit documented acceptance. MINOR issues may ship.

## BLOCKER (launch-blocking)
Private Alpha cannot ship with any open blocker.
- Plugin fails to load
- Crash on panel open or preset apply
- Black / corrupt frames
- Transform incorrect (wrong A/B, wrong units)
- Preset applies wrong units
- Project persistence broken
- Native/panel contract mismatch (binding revision or parameter count)

## MAJOR (requires documented acceptance)
- One timing edge case broken
- Undo grouping imperfect
- Multi-selection unreliable

## MINOR (may ship)
- UI spacing
- Preview mismatch (cosmetic)
- Wording

## Gate tracking
Open gates are tracked per `src/domain/releaseGates.ts` and summarized in the
host-status ledger. As of this milestone, **all host-dependent gates are open
and NOT YET TESTED** — they become BLOCKER-class until operator-verified or
explicitly accepted as MAJOR/MINOR with written rationale.

## Acceptance rule
A gate downgraded from BLOCKER to MAJOR must record: who accepted, the risk,
and the fallback if it fails in the field.
